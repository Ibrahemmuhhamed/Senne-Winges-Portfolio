What I can do for you

Product Discovery

I help you understand the customer problem at hand, generate possible directions, and make ideas tangible for validation. The result enables you to make more confident business decisions and make sure you build the right thing.

Product Design

I design digital products that connect customer needs with business goals in a meaningful way. I create screens, flows, design systems, and prototypes using UX and UI design to make products come to life.

Product Leadership

I lead and guide interdisciplinary teams to do the best work possible. My goal is to find the right balance between the product, processes, and people. To create a clear focus, I use a product strategy to inform what to build and why.

Nice to meet you

I am Sanne Wijbenga, Dutch designer and creative director with 20 years of experience. For over a decade, I worked at agencies in The Netherlands and Germany, creating award-winning work for a broad range of clients across industries. I love working with decision-makers on the intersection of design, strategy, and business. I believe this is where design can have the biggest impact. In 2018 I started my independent design practice using my experience to help early-stage startups create high-quality products that customers love and let businesses thrive. I'm based in Munich and collaborate with clients across Europe.

Working together

I follow a pragmatic approach where we combine your knowledge of your customer, product, and industry with my design expertise. I work with quick check-ins instead of big presentations.

Making things tangible fast creates clarity and sparks discussion, helping us identify risks and uncover potential.

Where to start

  • Chemistry Project

    The easiest way to start working together and experience what value I can bring to your business. The starting point can either be a predefined design challenge or a joined deep dive where we uncover opportunities. At the end of the two weeks, you will have concrete design solutions.

      Great For

    • Product Discovery Work
    • Startups With a clear commitment to improve their product

  • Expert Review

    The smallest step to get a taste of my added value. I will suggest how to improve your product using my expertise and best practice research. You will receive a presentation and backlog with possible improvements and opportunity areas.

      Great For

    • Small startups with an existing product and limited budget

  • Monthly Retainer

    A monthly retainer might be for you if you are looking for steady support over an extended period. We can focus on hands-on design or the management of your design or product team.

      Great For

    • Startups with a clear roadmap
    • Scale-ups with an existing design team
